PROMPT: Anticipate Objections & Counter-Arguments (Devil's Advocate)

Act as defense counsel for the defendant. Your job is to ATTACK our case.

ANALYZE AND IDENTIFY:
1. Every weakness in the complaint
2. Every available defense
3. Every procedural motion to dismiss/delay
4. Every affirmative defense
5. Every factual dispute
6. Every legal vulnerability

THRESHOLD DEFENSES:
- Lack of standing
- Lack of jurisdiction  
- Improper venue
- Statute of limitations
- Failure to state a claim
- Arbitration clause

AFFIRMATIVE DEFENSES:
- Waiver, estoppel, laches
- Failure to mitigate
- Contributory negligence
- Assumption of risk
- Payment, release

For each objection/defense:
1. The argument they'll make
2. Case law they'll cite
3. OUR counter-argument
4. Case law WE'll cite
5. Likelihood of success (%)
6. How to neutralize this defense

Generate 3-steps-ahead strategy showing our move, their response, our counter.
