PROMPT: Service of Process Address Compilation

Research and compile all addresses for service of process and legal correspondence:

1. REGISTERED AGENT (primary service)
   - Name and company
   - Street address (no PO boxes)
   - State of registration
   - Backup agents

2. CORPORATE HEADQUARTERS
   - Principal place of business
   - Mailing address if different

3. KEY EXECUTIVES
   - CEO office address
   - General Counsel address
   - CFO address

4. CUSTOMER SERVICE ADDRESSES
   - Refund department
   - Dispute resolution
   - Legal notice address (per ToS)

5. REGULATORY ADDRESSES
   - SEC filing address (if public)
   - State registration address

Verify all addresses through Secretary of State, company website, SEC filings, and court records.
