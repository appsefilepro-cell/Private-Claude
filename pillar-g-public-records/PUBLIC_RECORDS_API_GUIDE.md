## FREE Public Records Services

### 1. Secretary of State Business Searches

#### California Secretary of State
- **Website:** https://businesssearch.sos.ca.gov/
- **Cost:** **FREE**
- **Data Available:**
  - Business entity name and number
  - Entity type (LLC, Corp, Partnership)
  - Status (Active, Suspended, Dissolved)
  - Registration date
  - Agent for service of process name and address
  - Business address
  - Officers/directors (for corporations)
- **API:** No official API - web scraping possible
- **Limitations:** No bulk downloads
- **Best For:** Verifying California business entities and registered agents

#### Texas Secretary of State (SOSDirect)
- **Website:** https://www.sos.texas.gov/corp/sosda/index.shtml
- **Cost:** **FREE** for basic searches
- **Data Available:**
  - Entity information
  - Assumed names (DBAs)
  - UCC filings
  - Tax franchise reports
- **API:** Limited API access via SOSDirect system
- **Nonprofit Access:** Free public access (no special discount needed)

#### Wyoming Secretary of State
- **Website:** https://wyobiz.wyo.gov/Business/FilingSearch.aspx
- **Cost:** **FREE**
- **Data Available:**
  - Business name, status, entity type
  - Formation date, annual reports
  - Registered agent
- **Best For:** Wyoming entity verification (APPS Holdings WY Inc.)

### 3. Court Records - PACER

#### PACER (Public Access to Court Electronic Records)
- **Website:** https://pacer.uscourts.gov/
- **Regular Cost:** $0.10 per page (max $3 per document)
- **Fee Waiver:** **YES - Available for 501(c)(3) nonprofits**
- **How to Apply for Fee Waiver:**
  1. Download Form AO 239: "Application for Exemption from PACER Fees"
  2. Available at: https://www.pacer.gov/help/pacer-billing/fee-exemption.html
  3. Complete form with:
     - Organization name and EIN
     - 501(c)(3) determination letter (attach copy)
     - Explanation of nonprofit purpose
     - Statement that PACER use is for nonprofit purposes
  4. Mail to: PACER Service Center, P.O. Box 780549, San Antonio, TX 78278
  5. Or email scanned copy to: pacer@psc.uscourts.gov
  6. Processing time: 2-4 weeks
- **Data Available:** All federal court dockets and documents
- **Best For:** Federal civil rights cases, elder abuse (if prosecuted federally)

## Sheriff Service of Process Integration

### Harris County Sheriff's Office (Houston, TX)

**Service:** Civil Process Division
**Website:** https://www.harriscountyso.org/
**Phone:** (713) 755-5000

**Process Serving Costs:**
- **Regular Service:** $75 per defendant
- **Rush Service:** $100 per defendant (3-5 days)
- **Standard Timeline:** 7-10 business days

**Fee Waiver Process:**
1. Obtain court-approved fee waiver (pauper's affidavit) from Harris County court
2. File "Affidavit of Inability to Pay" with clerk
3. If approved, present waiver to sheriff with documents
4. Sheriff serves at no cost

**How to Submit for Service:**
- **Online:** https://civilprocessonline.harriscountyso.org/ (requires account)
- **In Person:** 1200 Baker Street, Houston, TX 77002
- **Documents Required:**
  - Original summons/citation
  - Original petition/complaint
  - Copy for defendant
  - Service instructions form
  - Payment or fee waiver certificate

**Tracking:** Use online portal with case number or call (713) 755-6044

**Proof of Service:** Certified return of service mailed to court and petitioner within 5 days of service

### Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department

**Service:** Civil Management Bureau
**Website:** https://lasd.org/civil/
**Phone:** (323) 526-5541

**Process Serving Costs:**
- **Summons & Complaint:** $40 first page + $10 each additional defendant
- **Subpoena:** $75
- **Writ of Execution:** $150

**Fee Waiver Process:**
1. File FW-001 (Fee Waiver Application) with LA Superior Court
2. Include income verification (pay stubs, benefit letters)
3. If approved, clerk stamps "Approved for Fee Waiver"
4. Present stamped documents to sheriff with service request
5. Sheriff serves at no cost

**How to Submit:**
- **Online:** https://lasd.org/civil/ (e-filing portal)
- **In Person:** 211 W. Temple Street, Los Angeles, CA 90012
- **By Mail:** LASD Civil, 211 W. Temple St., Los Angeles, CA 90012

**Timeline:** 15-30 days standard; expedited service available for additional fee

**Certified Mail Alternative (Much Cheaper):**
- **USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt:** $4-$8 per mailing
- **Allowed for:** Some document types (check court rules)
- **Tracking:** USPS.com with tracking number
- **No Fee Waiver:** Not government service, must pay
- **Proof of Service:** Green card return receipt + declaration of mailing (fill out yourself)

## Nonprofit Documentation Package

### Documents to Prepare for Discount Applications

1. **IRS 501(c)(3) Determination Letter** (if applicable)
   - Shows tax-exempt status
   - Provides EIN

2. **State Nonprofit Registration**
   - California: AG Registry of Charities
   - Texas: Secretary of State nonprofit filing

3. **EIN Confirmation Letter** (Form CP 575)
   - Issued by IRS when EIN assigned

4. **Organizational Documents**
   - Articles of Incorporation
   - Bylaws
   - Mission statement

5. **Current Year Financial Statement**
   - Shows nonprofit is active and in good standing
