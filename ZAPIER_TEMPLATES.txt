GOOGLE SHEETS FORMULAS
=======================

Use these formulas in your Google Sheets for automatic calculations.

1. Total P&L:
=SUM(G2:G)

2. Win Rate:
=COUNTIF(G2:G,">0")/COUNTA(G2:G)*100

3. Average Trade Size:
=AVERAGE(D2:D)

4. Total Trades Today:
=COUNTIF(A2:A,TODAY())

5. Best Trade:
=MAX(G2:G)

6. Worst Trade:
=MIN(G2:G)

7. Total Volume:
=SUMPRODUCT(D2:D,E2:E)

AIRTABLE VIEWS
==============

Set up these views in Airtable for better organization.

1. Legal Cases - Active Cases:
Filter: Status = "Active"
Sort: Deadline (ascending)

2. Legal Cases - Upcoming Deadlines:
Filter: Deadline is within the next 7 days
Sort: Deadline (ascending)

3. Financial Operations - This Month:
Filter: Date is within this month
Group by: Category

4. Trading - Winning Trades:
Filter: P&L > 0
Sort: P&L (descending)

5. System Monitoring - Active Errors:
Filter: Status = "New" OR Status = "In Progress"
Sort: Severity (HIGH first)

ZAPIER FILTER CONDITIONS
=========================

Use these filter conditions in your Zaps (Zapier Paths).

1. Only High Severity Errors:
Field: Severity
Condition: Exactly matches
Value: HIGH

2. Only Profitable Trades:
Field: P&L
Condition: Greater than
Value: 0

3. Only Work Hours:
Field: Timestamp
Condition: Time of day is between
Value: 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM

4. Only Weekdays:
Field: Timestamp
Condition: Day of week
Value: Monday through Friday
