---

### III. CONDITIONS & LIMITATIONS

This pro tanto distribution is subject to the following conditions:

1. **No Prejudice:** This order does not prejudice the rights of any creditor who files a valid claim before the claim filing deadline.

2. **Recapture Provision:** If additional creditor claims are allowed, the Personal Representative may seek recapture of distributed funds from beneficiaries.

3. **Receipt & Refunding Bond:** Beneficiary receiving distribution must execute a Receipt and Refunding Bond (attached as Exhibit A).

4. **Final Accounting:** This distribution is partial only; final distribution will be made after Court approval of final accounting.

---

### IV. NO DISCHARGE

This order **DOES NOT** constitute final discharge of the Personal Representative. The Personal Representative shall continue to:

1. Preserve remaining estate assets
2. Pay ongoing expenses and claims
3. Prepare and file final accounting
4. Request final distribution and discharge

---
